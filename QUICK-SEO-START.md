# Quick SEO Start Guide

A simplified, step-by-step guide to get started with SEO immediately.

## üéØ Today's Tasks (30 minutes)

### 1. Google Search Console Setup (15 minutes)

1. **Go to**: https://search.google.com/search-console
2. **Sign in** with your Google account
3. **Add Property**:
   - Click "Add Property"
   - Select "URL prefix"
   - Enter: `https://thenamegenerators.com`
   - Click "Continue"
4. **Verify Ownership**:
   - Choose "HTML tag" method (easiest)
   - Copy the meta tag provided
   - I'll help you add it to your site
   - Click "Verify"
5. **Submit Sitemap**:
   - Go to "Sitemaps" in left menu
   - Enter: `sitemap.xml`
   - Click "Submit"

### 2. Google Analytics Setup (10 minutes)

1. **Go to**: https://analytics.google.com
2. **Create Account** (if you don't have one)
3. **Create Property**:
   - Property name: "The Name Generators"
   - Time zone: Your timezone
   - Currency: USD
4. **Get Tracking Code**:
   - Copy the Measurement ID (G-XXXXXXXXXX)
   - I'll help you add it to your site

### 3. Test Your Site (5 minutes)

1. **Page Speed**: https://pagespeed.web.dev
   - Enter: `https://thenamegenerators.com`
   - Check scores (aim for 90+)
2. **Mobile-Friendly**: https://search.google.com/test/mobile-friendly
   - Enter: `https://thenamegenerators.com`
   - Should pass
3. **Schema Validator**: https://validator.schema.org
   - Enter any generator page URL
   - Should validate
