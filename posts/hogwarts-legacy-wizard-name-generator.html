Name Styles by House

Gryffindor names emphasize bravery and adventure (Arthur Stormheart, Cedric Ironwill, Gideon Redthorne). They often incorporate fire, storms, lions, and elements that suggest courage. Last names suggest strength and heroism (Stormheart, Ironwill, Redthorne).

Ravenclaw names feature scholarly and mystical elements (Silas Evermere, Phineas Nightvale, Augustus Silverwood). They may reference stars, wisdom, or elemental magic. Last names suggest places of learning or intellectual pursuits (Evermere, Nightvale, Silverwood).
