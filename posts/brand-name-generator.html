What makes a good brand name?

A good brand name is memorable, easy to pronounce, reflects your brand values and market position, appeals to your target audience, works well for branding and marketing, and stands out in the competitive market. It should communicate your company's personality and character in just one word.

Can I use these names for my actual business?

Yes, but always check trademark availability, domain availability, and business registration availability before using any name for your actual business. These names are provided as inspiration and starting points for your own creative naming process.
