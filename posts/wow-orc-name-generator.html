Orc Classes and Naming Patterns

World of Warcraft orc names are organized by class, reflecting the diverse roles orcs play in Horde society. Warriors have names that emphasize strength, honor, and combat prowess. Names like Gromtar, Thrallok, and Zugul suggest frontline fighters who lead the charge into battle.

Shamans have names connected to the elements, spirits, and the natural world. Names like Drogar, Volgrin, and Lokgor reflect their connection to the spiritual realm and their role as mediators between the orc people and the elements.

Rogues have names suggesting stealth, cunning, and precision. Names like Rugth, Ulgash, and Throk reflect their role as scouts, assassins, and shadowy operatives who work from the shadows.

Chieftains have names that command respect and authority. Names like Ghash, Thrak, and Boruk reflect their leadership role and their responsibility for their clan or tribe.

Berserkers have names that reflect their fury, rage, and unstoppable nature. Names like Drogoth, Rhazgar, and Brogna suggest warriors who fight with wild abandon and overwhelming ferocity.
