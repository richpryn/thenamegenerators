Name Styles by House

Ravenclaw names often feature celestial or scholarly elements (Isolde Ashwood, Cassiopeia Eldenbrook, Seraphina Vexley). They may reference stars, wisdom, or mystical knowledge. Last names often suggest places of learning or intellectual pursuits (Eldenbrook, Whitethorne, Inkwood).

Hufflepuff names emphasize warmth and nature (Olive Starling, Juniper Honeywick, Primrose Goodfellow). They often use plant names, gentle sounds, and references to the natural world. Last names suggest cozy, welcoming places (Meadowglen, Honeywick, Goodfellow).

Gryffindor names suggest strength and courage (Medea Thornfield, Bellatrix Raventhorne, Selene Darkmoore). They often have bold, powerful sounds and may reference battle, honor, or adventure. Last names suggest strength and determination (Thornfield, Raventhorne, Darkmoore).

Slytherin names have a darker, more mysterious quality (Calypso Darkstream, Roxana Venomshade, Bellatrix Darkmist). They often incorporate shadow, venom, or cold elements. Last names suggest darkness, power, or cunning (Darkstream, Venomshade, Darkmist).
